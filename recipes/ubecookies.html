Ube cookies

Vegetable in a cookie, cookie an healthy. Make no mistake this tasty treat does have a vegetable, but it will make you rethink what is possible with things that are considered healthy. Do not get it wrong it is still a cookie and has a lot of sugar, but you have to enjoy a treat every now and then. Why not have your treat the color purple naturally from a purple yam

Ingredints

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅔ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ⅓ cup purple yam powder, or more to taste
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  1. Preheat oven to 400 deg F
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, grease lightly
  3. Mix sugar, oil, eggs, vanilla extract, salt, baking powder, purple yam powder and flour, in this order, until dough is well blended
  4. Drop tablespoons onto a plate and put in freezer for 10 mins to allow for cookies to be cold and prevent spreading to much in oven
  5. Bake cookies until they look firm 8-12 minutes and cool for at least 5 minutes before placing on wire rack
