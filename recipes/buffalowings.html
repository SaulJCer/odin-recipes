Buffalo wings recipe

These delicious wings can be made from the comfort of your home. They use a spciy but manageable sauce. Use the oven to cook them and finish on the stove top to get a nice and crispy skin. I love buffalo wings and if you enjoy wings, you will love this recipe.

Ingredints
  • 3 pounds chicken wings, separated at joints, tips discarded
  • 1 cup Louisiana-style hot sauce
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle cola-flavored carbonated beverage
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Pat dry wings and season wings with desried spices
  3. Place them in oven preheated oven for 30 Minutes, flipping half way though
  4. Lower oven to 200 degrees F and cover wings with sauce and put on a pan to get crisp edges
  5. While cooking wings in pan, put finsihed wings back in the oven to stay warm
  6. Serve with nice crispy fries and enjoy
